A list of readily-available volunteer opportunities in the Saint Louis area is kept up-to-date [in this Google Sheet](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1--oVVsDkIoh7D18tMZ9OP-fRUpROl6rM/edit?usp=sharing&ouid=108699184310571704858&rtpof=true&sd=true). Opportunities highlighted light blue are annually-recurring volunteer events set up specially for The Secret Service Project. Enlist your friends and sign up before spots fill up!

The list is made available only for your convenience; participants are encouraged to volunteer anywhere that interests them! It can be with a nonprofit, or something as simple as picking up trash or helping a neighbor in need.
