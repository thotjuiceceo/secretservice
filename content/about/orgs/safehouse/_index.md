Serving the Saint Louis since 1977, [The Women's Safe House](https://twsh.org/) empowers women and families to rebuild their lives free of domestic and sexual abuse through emergency services, education and advocacy. The Women's Safe House and Secret Service partner annually to provide and fulfill volunteer needs!

## Secret Service 2025
Last year, fifteen Secret Service volunteers supported The Women's Safe House by sorting donations, curating care packages, and keeping their facilities clean!
